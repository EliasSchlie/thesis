## Writing guidelines
Follow those if I ask you to write a text for me

- Only change the text that I ask you to change (nothing else!)

### Style (combine the following)
- Try to keep my tone of voice and writing style from other sections.
- Use a spartan tone of voice
- Don't write in the passive voice
- Be as clear and concise as possible
- Prioritize words and sentences that are easy to understand
- In non-academic writing: Emphasize important parts with bold/italic font.

### Formatting
- Whenever it doesn't make the rendered output look weird (e.g., latex, HTML), write a newline for every new sentence.

## Rewriting guidelines
Follow those steps if I ask you to rewrite a text

Let the rewriting process be guided by the following questions, but be extremely careful that we don't lose any information during rewriting. When in doubt, better leave it as is.
- Is there a way to make it simpler/easier to understand?
- Is there a way to say the same thing with fewer words?
- Does the writing follow all writing guidelines from above?
